Hailo was one of apps that connected passengers and taxi drivers. Hailo was launched in London and later was launched in NYC. The difference in London was that taxi drivers were more trained and professional as opposed to NYC where those did not have smartphones. So, the different culture in NYC is one of reasons that failed Hailo. To add another issue to it, Uber started service way cheaper.
